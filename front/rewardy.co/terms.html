Rewardy Services

Rewardy is a mobile communication platform that uses a chat bot "Rewardy" to help you change unhealthy eating habits by keeping a virtual food diary for you and sharing it with your friends and/or professional trainer/nutrition consultant ("trainer"). These Services allow you to communicate with the chat bot by sending a text message or a photo describing the food or drinks you have consumed throughout the day. The chat bot will react to your messages depending on what you specified as your desired goal; e.g. to gain weight, lose weight, or just healthy eating. Your virtual food diary will be formed based on your messages sent to the chat bot, and will be available to you at any time. You can also share your virtual food diary with your friends or your trainer who will be able to comment on your eating habits and adjust your diet accordingly. Our chat bot will communicate with you in an easy and friendly manner. For example, if you mentioned that you ate a high-calorie dessert for dinner, our chat bot may answer: " Your favorite jeans aren't going to fit if you keep eating like that." If this manner of communication bothers you, please refrain from using our Services.
